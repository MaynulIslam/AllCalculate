How Age is Calculated

Age calculation is more nuanced than simply subtracting years. This calculator accounts for the exact number of days in each month, leap years, and the day-of-month boundary.

Exact Age: Compare year, month, and day components
Total Days: (As-Of Date − Birth Date) in milliseconds ÷ 86,400,000
Total Weeks: Math.floor(totalDays / 7)
Total Months: (years × 12) + remaining months

Age in Different Cultures

  • Western: Age increments on your birthday each year.
  • East Asian (Traditional): You are born at age 1, and gain a year on Lunar New Year — so you may be 1–2 years "older" by this system.
  • Korean System: Similar to East Asian traditional counting.

Leap Year Considerations

People born on February 29 (leap day) officially celebrate their birthday on February 28 or March 1 in non-leap years, depending on convention. This calculator uses February 28 as the observed birthday in non-leap years for counting purposes.

Fun Age Facts

  • The oldest verified human ever was Jeanne Calment of France, who lived to 122 years, 164 days.
  • By age 70, the average person has slept roughly 230,000 hours (about 26 years).
  • Your heart beats approximately 100,000 times per day — over 3.6 billion times in a lifetime.
